About Us

Our Vision and Mission

Our vision is to cultivate a dynamic community of artists and art enthusiasts, where creativity flourishes and diverse perspectives are celebrated. Through our exhibitions, events, and educational programs, we aim to promote artistic excellence, foster innovation, and enrich the cultural landscape of our city.

Our Values

Kouros-Kore is a leading contemporary art gallery dedicated to showcasing the work of emerging and established artists. Located in Bucharest, our gallery offers a dynamic space for artists and art enthusiasts to connect, engage, and explore the power of contemporary art. With a commitment to excellence, diversity, accessibility, and collaboration, Kouros-Kore is at the forefront of shaping the cultural landscape and fostering a vibrant arts community.

Out Team

Behind Kouros-Kore is a passionate team of curators, art professionals, and dedicated staff members who are committed to supporting artists and promoting the arts. Meet our team and learn more about the individuals who make our gallery a vibrant hub of creativity and innovation.
